A little context

I’m not trying to reinvent photography. I’m just collecting the small, cinematic moments that make regular life feel like a strange little movie.

The vibe
Moody but not depressing. Playful but not chaotic. Think: if your favorite overcast day and a weirdly comforting dream moved in together.
  • Color palettes inspired by rainy PNW days & old movie posters.
  • A mix of film grain, soft focus, and clean lines — nothing too glossy.
  • Images that look good solo or in little story clusters on your wall.
How prints work
Small studio, slow pace, high-quality prints.
  • Printed on archival, matte paper (no glare, just vibes).
  • Multiple sizes available — from “tiny altar” to “statement wall”.
  • Packed simply but safely, because we respect the mail journey.
